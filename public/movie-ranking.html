The Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all-time.

There Will Be Blood

This is my favorite drama of all time. It contains very pensive moments with religious dilemmas

National Treasure

This is a feel good adventure movie that's enjoyable for all ages. Nicholas Cage steals the show

Dune

A truly epic action movie that displays the capabilities of modern-day cinema. Enjoy the action and multiple rewatches for the political intrigue
